### Detailed description
Send& adds a simple “Send&” button to Gmail that lets you:

- **Send & Follow Up**: schedule a follow-up email for a chosen date/time. If someone replies in the thread before then, the follow-up is cancelled.\n+- **Send & Remind Me**: schedule a reminder email back to yourself at a chosen date/time (with an optional note).\n+\n+Send& uses Google OAuth and the Gmail API to send scheduled messages and detect replies.\n+\n+### Requirements\n+- A Gmail / Google Workspace account.\n+- You must connect your Gmail account in Send& Settings.\n+\n+### Privacy\n+Send& requires Gmail API access to send scheduled emails and check for replies.
